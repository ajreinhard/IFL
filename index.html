IFL Elo Projections

Welcome! This page is dedicated IFL Elo rankings. The methodology I'm using is based on FiveThirtyEight's pre-2019 NFL elo model. Pre-season ratings were computed based on IFL Elo ratings from prior seasons. The difference between two team's ratings is equal to the point spread between those two teams. The home team will also get a 2.3 point home-field advantage. An average team will have a rating of zero. Projections below are based on 10,000 simulations. This page will be updated as games are played.
